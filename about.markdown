# Hi! I'm Paulena Prevatt
My career has been shaped by two things: service and systems. I spent more than a decade in the U.S. Army, where I learned how to lead, adapt, and stay mission‑focused under pressure. That experience continues to guide me today in my role as a Drug Testing Technician for the Army Substance Abuse Program, supporting soldier wellness and organizational readiness.
Along the way, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology, which deepened my understanding of people and behavior. As my career evolved, I discovered a passion for technology and cybersecurity — the systems that keep organizations running and information protected. That led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems, where I’ve excelled academically with multiple Provost’s List recognitions.

Now, I’m focused on building a career that blends my strengths: technical problem‑solving, human insight, and the discipline of military service. I’m especially interested in cybersecurity, IT security management, and roles that support secure, resilient operations in military or government environments.
